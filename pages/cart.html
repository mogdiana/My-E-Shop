Returning an item

Due to current restrictions, we have extended our return policy from 14 days to 45 days from your delivery date.
Step 1: To return your unwanted items, please mark on your delivery note which you would like to return using the reason codes provided.
Step 2: Return your items in the original packaging (if possible), with the tags still attached. We're unable to accept returns for any pierced jewellery, cosmetics, perfume where the cellophane packaging has been removed or any underwear or swimwear where the hygiene seal has been removed. All of our face coverings are non-returnable.
Step 3: Download and print a return label and return at your nearest post office. 4 euros will be deducted from your refund
